What if your worst nightmare became your greatest ally? Paige and Wyatt don’t know the other exists until they inherit the same house and discover they share the same dreams. Working together, they unlock a portal to the realm of Ro’an that has imprisoned their long-lost father. When their nightmare shows up for real, it delivers a message that sends them on a mission to both rescue the realm from a growing evil and find their father. While being hunted by the same people they’re trying to save, they traverse the strange and mystical land, discover strengths within themselves, and begin trusting that love and the family you build around you is the strongest power there is. Will they find the solution in time?

ABOUT THE AUTHORS

Find Us On These Platforms!

Dan Wilson started his writing career as a close-up magician – thinking of the impossible and doing it with a deck of cards. By his mid-twenties, the cards were replaced with words, and his heroes and heroines were achieving the impossible.

After writing features for New Line Cinema, Paramount, and Sony Animation; Dan branched out into video game writing as well. He drafted plot and dialogue for Sony Online Entertainment, EA Games, and Ubisoft. Then, after several years of writing for Disney’s feature department, Dan dove headfirst into designing escape rooms and ended up with eight story-driven rooms under his belt, one of which received the Golden Lock Award. During the pandemic he and his sisters, Stephanie and Denise, completed their first fiction novel.

During the editing process, Dan began to explore the world of NFTs. And soon, he began creating worlds, stories, puzzles, and lore for the NFT community – the perfect outlet for his lifelong passion of conjuring wonder for his audience.

Denise’s laugh can be heard across the room. She loves to tell stories and extrapolate reality into a fun weave of fantasy, magic, and adventure.

Hayes

Wilson Bendrick Hayes is a nom de plume that is a story in and of itself.

Dan Wilson, Stephanie Bendrick, and Denise Hayes are Air Force siblings who grew up constantly relocating. Friends and homes came and went, but their one consistency was a strong family bond. As they came of age, all three went their separate ways – Dan to Los Angeles, Stephanie to Colorado with her husband, and Denise continued to travel with her military spouse and family. Each sibling pursued a different career path, and their personalities grew in vastly different directions.

One fateful holiday, Denise’s children inspired Stephanie and Denise to propose an idea to their brother, a screenwriter for major studios by trade. By nightfall, the three conjured up a children’s story concept that later evolved into a young adult novel. The three of them attended seminars, outlined story, wrote rough drafts, edited, and rewrote. They used their unique perspectives as tools and shared childhood passions as glue, pushing through the barriers. Mirroring the theme of Darkender and Lightgiver, the power of family came together – Wilson Bendrick Hayes became an unstoppable union.

Inspired by their experience, Denise achieved her master’s in creative writing, Stephanie’s keen editorial eye blossomed, and Dan, having added more screenplays and video games to his resume, tackled his greatest challenge – the novel.

Forged in the intersection of sibling rivalry and childhood comradery, the first novel in the Darkender and Lightgiver series is now at hand.
